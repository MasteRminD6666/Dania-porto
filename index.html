What Does a Pharmacist Do?

Dispensing Medications

ensure the accurate preparation and distribution of prescribed medications, providing vital instructions for proper use.

Patient Care

offer counseling to patients on the safe and effective use of medications, including managing side effects and drug interactions.

Compounding Medications

prepare custom medications tailored to the unique needs of patients when standard treatments aren't sufficient.

Health Screenings

perform basic health screenings such as blood pressure checks, diabetes monitoring, and vaccinations.

Medication Reviews

review patients' medication regimens to ensure optimal therapy and prevent drug interactions or overuse.

Collaborating with Healthcare Teams

work closely with doctors, nurses, and other professionals to provide the best possible patient care.

Promoting Wellness

Pharmacists educate the community on wellness and preventive care, emphasizing the importance of healthy lifestyles.

Research and Innovation

Pharmacists contribute to the development of new medications and therapies through research and clinical trials.
